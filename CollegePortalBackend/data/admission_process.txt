COLLEGE ADMISSION POLICY
Admission Overview

The college offers admissions to Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) programs based on eligibility criteria established by the academic council. Students must complete every stage of the admission process before the specified deadlines.

Admissions are conducted once every academic year, and all applicants are advised to regularly check the official college website for announcements and updates.

Eligibility Criteria
Undergraduate Programs

Applicants must:

Have successfully completed the 12th Standard (Higher Secondary Examination).
Meet the minimum percentage required for the selected course.
Possess valid educational certificates from a recognized board.
Postgraduate Programs

Applicants must:

Hold a Bachelor's degree in a relevant discipline.
Meet the minimum qualifying marks specified by the department.
Submit all required academic transcripts during admission.
Required Documents

Students seeking admission must submit the following documents:

10th Marksheet
12th Marksheet
Transfer Certificate
Passport Size Photographs
Aadhaar Card Copy

Additional documents, if applicable:

Caste Certificate
Income Certificate
Migration Certificate
Character Certificate
Gap Certificate (if applicable)
Domicile Certificate
Entrance Examination Score Card

All documents should be original along with two sets of self-attested photocopies.

Admission Process

The admission process consists of the following steps:

Step 1 – Online Registration

Applicants must create an account using a valid email address and mobile number.

During registration, students must:

Fill personal information
Provide academic details
Select the preferred course
Upload required documents

Step 2 – Document Verification

The admission office verifies submitted documents.

Verification includes:

Educational qualification
Identity verification
Category certificates
Uploaded documents

Students may be asked to visit the campus if physical verification is required.

Step 3 – Merit List Publication

After verification, the college publishes a merit list based on:

Academic performance
Entrance examination score (if applicable)
Reservation policies
Seat availability

Students whose names appear on the merit list become eligible for admission.

Step 4 – Fee Payment

Selected candidates must pay the admission fee within the specified deadline.

Payment methods include:

Online Banking
Debit Card
Credit Card
UPI
Net Banking

Failure to pay the fee before the deadline may result in cancellation of admission.

Step 5 – Enrollment Confirmation

After successful fee payment, students receive:

Enrollment Number
Student ID
College Email Account
Access to the Student Portal

The admission process is considered complete after enrollment confirmation.

Admission Timeline
Online Registration Opens: May 15
Registration Closes: August 20
Document Verification: June to August
Merit List Publication: August 25
Fee Payment Deadline: August 30
Admissions Close: August 31
Admission Cancellation Policy

A student's admission may be cancelled if:

False information is provided.
Required documents are not submitted.
Admission fee is not paid before the deadline.
Eligibility requirements are not satisfied.

Students failing to submit required documents before the deadline may lose their admission seat.

Reservation Policy

Admissions are conducted according to government reservation guidelines.

Reserved categories may include:

SC
ST
OBC
EWS
PwD

Applicants claiming reservation benefits must provide valid supporting documents.

Scholarships During Admission

Eligible students can apply for:

Merit Scholarship
Government Scholarship
Economically Weaker Section Scholarship
Minority Scholarship

Scholarship applications must be submitted separately after admission confirmation.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the last date for admission?

Admissions close on August 31 every year.

Can I submit documents after the deadline?

No. Late submissions are generally not accepted unless officially announced by the college.

Can someone else complete document verification on my behalf?

No. The applicant should personally complete the verification process unless exceptional circumstances are approved by the admission office.

Is the admission fee refundable?

Admission fees are refundable only according to the college's refund policy and applicable regulations.

How will I know if my admission is confirmed?

Students receive an Enrollment Confirmation along with their Student ID and official college email after successful fee payment.
