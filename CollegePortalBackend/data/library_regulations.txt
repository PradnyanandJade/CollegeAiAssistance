COLLEGE LIBRARY RULES AND REGULATIONS
Library Overview

The college library provides students and faculty members with access to academic books, journals, research papers, magazines, newspapers, and digital learning resources. The library is designed to support teaching, learning, research, and self-study.

Students are encouraged to make full use of the library facilities while maintaining discipline and following library regulations.

Library Working Hours

The college library remains open during the following hours:

Weekdays
Opening Time: 8:00 AM
Closing Time: 8:00 PM
Saturdays
9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Sundays and Public Holidays
Closed

During examination periods, the library timings may be extended based on the decision of the college administration.

Library Membership

Every enrolled student automatically becomes a library member after completing the admission process.

To use library services, students must possess:

Valid College Identity Card
Active Library Membership

Students must present their college ID card while borrowing books.

Book Borrowing Policy

Students may borrow:

Maximum 4 books at a time.
Borrowing period is 14 days.

Books should be returned on or before the due date to avoid late fees.

Borrowed books may be renewed if:

No reservation exists for the book.
The renewal request is made before the due date.
Late Fee Policy

Late returns attract the following penalty:

₹5 per day per book

The late fee continues until the borrowed book is returned.

Students with outstanding library fines may not be permitted to borrow additional books until all dues are cleared.

Reference Section

Reference books are meant only for use inside the library.

The following materials cannot be issued outside the library:

Encyclopedias
Dictionaries
Reference Books
Rare Books
Research Theses
Bound Journals

Students may use these materials within the library during working hours.

Digital Library Facilities

The library provides access to:

Digital Books (E-books)
Online Journals
Research Databases
Previous Year Question Papers
Project Reports
Institutional Repository

Students can access these resources using their college login credentials.

Reading Hall Rules

Students using the reading hall must:

Maintain complete silence.
Switch mobile phones to silent mode.
Keep the study area clean.
Respect other students' study environment.

Eating and drinking are not permitted inside the reading hall.

Computer and Internet Usage

Library computers are provided for academic purposes only.

Students may use them for:

Research
Online Learning
Accessing Digital Library Resources
Academic Assignments

Downloading unauthorized software or accessing inappropriate websites is strictly prohibited.

Library Rules

Students must:

Carry their college ID card while visiting the library.
Return books before the due date.
Handle books carefully.
Report damaged books immediately.
Maintain silence inside the library.
Keep personal belongings with them at all times.

Students should place borrowed books on the designated counter before leaving the library for verification.

Lost or Damaged Books

If a borrowed book is lost or damaged, the student must:

Replace the book with the latest edition, or
Pay the replacement cost along with any applicable processing charges.

Failure to replace the book may result in withholding academic documents until the matter is resolved.

Frequently Asked Questions
How many books can a student borrow?

Students may borrow a maximum of 4 books at a time.

What is the borrowing period?

Books can be borrowed for 14 days.

What is the late fee for overdue books?

The late fee is ₹5 per day per book.

Can reference books be borrowed?

No. Reference books cannot be issued outside the library.

Can I renew a borrowed book?

Yes. Books can be renewed if no reservation has been made by another student and the renewal request is submitted before the due date.

Is a college ID card required to borrow books?

Yes. Students must present their college ID card while borrowing books.
