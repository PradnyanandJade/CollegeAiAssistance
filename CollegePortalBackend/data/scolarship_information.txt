COLLEGE SCHOLARSHIP INFORMATION
Scholarship Overview

The college provides various scholarship programs to encourage academic excellence, support talented students, and assist economically disadvantaged students in pursuing higher education.

Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, sports achievements, financial background, and eligibility criteria defined by the college and government authorities.

Students are encouraged to apply before the specified deadlines and ensure that all required documents are submitted.

Merit Scholarship

The Merit Scholarship recognizes students who demonstrate outstanding academic performance.

Eligibility

Students must:

Have a CGPA above 9.0.
Maintain good academic standing.
Have no disciplinary actions against them.
Be enrolled as a full-time student.
Scholarship Amount
₹20,000

The scholarship is awarded once every academic year, subject to eligibility.

Sports Scholarship

The Sports Scholarship is awarded to students who have represented their state or country in recognized sporting events.

Eligibility

Students must:

Be State-Level or National-Level Athletes.
Submit valid sports achievement certificates.
Continue participating in college sports activities.
Maintain satisfactory academic performance.
Scholarship Amount
₹15,000

The scholarship may be renewed based on continued sports participation and performance.

Need-Based Scholarship

The Need-Based Scholarship supports students from economically weaker families.

Eligibility

Applicants must satisfy the following conditions:

Annual family income below ₹2,50,000.
Submit a valid income certificate issued by the competent authority.
Be enrolled as a regular student of the college.
Scholarship Amount
₹25,000

Financial assistance is subject to verification of submitted documents.

Government Scholarships

Eligible students may also apply for government-sponsored scholarship schemes through the college.

Examples include:

State Government Scholarship
Central Government Scholarship
Minority Scholarship
SC/ST Scholarship
OBC Scholarship
EWS Scholarship

Eligibility depends on the guidelines issued by the respective government authorities.

Scholarship Application Process

Students may apply through the Student Welfare Office before September 30.

The application process includes:

Collecting the scholarship application form.
Filling in personal and academic details.
Attaching all required supporting documents.
Submitting the application before the deadline.
Verification by the Student Welfare Office.
Approval by the Scholarship Committee.

Students are advised to retain a copy of the submitted application for future reference.

Required Documents

Applicants may be required to submit:

College Identity Card
Aadhaar Card Copy
Recent Passport Size Photographs
Previous Semester Marksheet
Income Certificate (for Need-Based Scholarship)
Sports Achievement Certificates (for Sports Scholarship)
Bank Account Details
Bonafide Certificate

Incomplete applications may be rejected during verification.

Scholarship Renewal

Scholarships may be renewed annually if students:

Continue to satisfy the eligibility criteria.
Maintain satisfactory academic performance.
Follow college rules and regulations.
Submit renewal applications before the specified deadline.

Failure to meet the renewal conditions may result in cancellation of scholarship benefits.

Scholarship Disbursement

Approved scholarship amounts are generally credited directly to the student's registered bank account.

Students should ensure that their bank account details are accurate and updated in the college records.

Scholarship Cancellation

A scholarship may be cancelled if:

False information is provided.
Supporting documents are found to be invalid.
Academic performance falls below the required standard.
The student violates college disciplinary rules.
Eligibility conditions are no longer satisfied.
