COLLEGE TRANSPORTATION SERVICES
Transportation Overview

The college provides safe and reliable transportation services for students and staff residing in different parts of the city. The transport facility is managed by the College Transport Department to ensure timely and secure travel between designated pickup points and the college campus.

Students wishing to use the transportation service must register with the Transport Office before the commencement of the academic year.

Bus Routes

The college operates buses on 12 routes across the city, covering major residential areas and nearby towns.

Bus routes are planned to ensure convenient access for students while minimizing travel time.

Students are advised to select the nearest pickup point during the registration process.

Bus Fee Structure

The transportation fee is charged annually.

Bus Fees
Annual Fee: ₹18,000

The transportation fee covers:

Morning Pickup
Evening Drop
Bus Maintenance
Driver and Support Staff Services

Students must pay the transport fee before the prescribed deadline to activate their bus pass.

Bus Timings

The standard transportation schedule is as follows:

Morning
Departure from designated pickup points: 7:30 AM
Evening
Return from college campus: 5:30 PM

Students are advised to arrive at their pickup point at least 10 minutes before the scheduled arrival time of the bus.

During examinations or special college events, bus timings may be revised by the Transport Department.

Bus Pass Policy

Students must carry a valid bus pass while traveling on college buses.

The bus pass contains:

Student Name
Enrollment Number
Bus Route Number
Pickup Point
Academic Year

Students may be asked to present their college ID card along with the bus pass for verification.

Lost Bus Pass

Lost bus passes can be reissued by paying ₹200.

To obtain a duplicate bus pass, students must:

Report the loss to the Transport Office.
Submit a duplicate bus pass application.
Pay the prescribed reissue fee.
Collect the new bus pass after verification.

Students are not permitted to travel without a valid bus pass.

Transportation Rules

Students using college buses must:

Reach the pickup point before the scheduled arrival time.
Follow the instructions of the bus driver and transport staff.
Occupy seats in an orderly manner.
Maintain discipline during travel.
Keep the bus clean and avoid littering.
Respect fellow passengers and staff.

Any damage caused to the bus property may result in disciplinary action and recovery of repair costs.

Safety Guidelines

For the safety of all passengers, students should:

Board and exit the bus only after it has come to a complete stop.
Avoid standing near the bus door while the vehicle is moving.
Never distract the driver during the journey.
Immediately report emergencies or unsafe situations to the driver or transport coordinator.

Emergency contact numbers are displayed inside every college bus.

Route Change Policy

Students wishing to change their assigned bus route must:

Submit a written request to the Transport Office.
Obtain approval from the Transport Officer.
Pay any additional charges if applicable.

Route changes are subject to seat availability.

Cancellation of Transport Facility

Students who no longer wish to use the transportation service must notify the Transport Office in writing.

Refunds, if applicable, are processed according to the college transportation refund policy.
