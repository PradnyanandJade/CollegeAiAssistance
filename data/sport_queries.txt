COLLEGE SPORTS FACILITIES AND POLICIES
Sports Overview

The college encourages students to actively participate in sports and physical fitness activities as part of their overall development. The Department of Physical Education organizes regular sports events, coaching sessions, inter-department competitions, and participation in university, state, and national tournaments.

Students are encouraged to maintain a healthy balance between academics and sports.

Sports Facilities

The college provides well-maintained facilities for the following sports:

Cricket
Football
Basketball
Volleyball
Badminton
Table Tennis

Additional recreational facilities include:

Indoor Gymnasium
Athletics Track
Chess Room
Carrom Room
Yoga Hall
Fitness Center

Students may use these facilities according to the schedule issued by the Sports Department.

Sports Complex Timings

The Sports Complex remains open during the following hours:

Opening Time: 6:00 AM
Closing Time: 9:00 PM

Students should vacate the sports complex before closing time unless participating in officially approved events or practice sessions.

Special timings may be announced during tournaments or college festivals.

Sports Membership

All enrolled students are eligible to use the sports facilities.

Students must:

Carry a valid College Identity Card.
Follow the instructions of coaches and sports staff.
Register for coaching sessions where applicable.
Use sports equipment responsibly.

Certain facilities may require prior booking during peak hours.

Sports Equipment

The college provides sports equipment for various games.

Available equipment includes:

Cricket Kits
Footballs
Basketballs
Volleyballs
Badminton Rackets and Shuttlecocks
Table Tennis Bats and Balls
Gym Equipment

Students borrowing sports equipment must return it in good condition after use.

Damage or loss of equipment may result in replacement charges.

Coaching and Training

Professional coaching is provided for selected sports throughout the academic year.

Training programs include:

Cricket Coaching
Football Practice
Basketball Training
Volleyball Coaching
Badminton Coaching
Athletics Training
Fitness and Strength Conditioning

Students interested in representing the college are encouraged to participate in regular practice sessions.

College Sports Teams

Students may be selected to represent the college in:

Inter-College Competitions
University Championships
State-Level Tournaments
National-Level Competitions

Selection is based on:

Skill Assessment
Fitness Level
Trial Performance
Discipline
Attendance during practice sessions
Attendance Concession

Students representing the college in university tournaments may receive attendance concessions, subject to approval by the Principal and the Academic Department.

Attendance concessions are granted only after:

Submission of participation certificates.
Verification by the Sports Department.
Approval from the concerned academic authorities.

Participation in unofficial tournaments does not qualify for attendance concessions.

Sports Scholarships

Outstanding sportspersons may be eligible for:

Sports Scholarships
Tournament Travel Assistance
Sports Excellence Awards
Recognition Certificates

Eligibility is determined by the Sports Committee and college administration.

Sports Rules

Students using sports facilities must:

Wear appropriate sports attire.
Use designated playing areas only.
Maintain discipline during practice sessions.
Respect coaches, officials, and fellow participants.
Return all borrowed equipment after use.
Keep the sports complex clean.

Students should report damaged equipment immediately to the Sports Department.

Safety Guidelines

Students are advised to:

Perform warm-up exercises before playing.
Use appropriate protective equipment where required.
Follow the instructions of coaches.
Immediately report any injuries to the sports staff.

The college provides basic first-aid facilities during official sports events.

Annual Sports Events

The college organizes several sporting events throughout the academic year, including:

Annual Sports Meet
Inter-Department Sports Championship
Freshers' Sports Competition
University Tournament Selections
Fitness Challenges
Intramural Competitions

Students from all departments are encouraged to participate.

Frequently Asked Questions
Which sports facilities are available in the college?

The college provides facilities for Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, and Table Tennis, along with a gymnasium, athletics track, yoga hall, and indoor games.

What are the sports complex timings?

The Sports Complex is open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Can students borrow sports equipment?

Yes. Students may borrow sports equipment and must return it in good condition after use.

Can students receive attendance concessions for sports participation?

Yes. Students representing the college in university tournaments may receive attendance concessions, subject to approval by the college authorities.

Does the college provide sports coaching?

Yes. Professional coaching and regular practice sessions are conducted for various sports throughout the academic year.

Are sports scholarships available?

Yes. Eligible students with outstanding sports achievements may receive sports scholarships and other recognition from the college.
