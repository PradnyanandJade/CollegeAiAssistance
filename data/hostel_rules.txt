COLLEGE HOSTEL RULES AND REGULATIONS
Hostel Overview

The college provides separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls. Hostel accommodation is available to students enrolled in full-time academic programs and is allotted based on availability and eligibility.

The hostel aims to provide a safe, comfortable, and disciplined environment that supports students' academic and personal development.

Hostel Eligibility

Students applying for hostel accommodation must:

Be enrolled as a full-time student of the college.
Complete the hostel application process.
Submit all required hostel documents.
Pay the hostel admission and security fees before the prescribed deadline.

Preference may be given to outstation students and those residing far from the college campus.

Hostel Fees

Annual hostel fees are as follows:

First Year Students: ₹45,000 per year
Second Year Students: ₹42,000 per year
Third Year Students: ₹40,000 per year
Fourth Year Students: ₹40,000 per year

The hostel fee includes:

Accommodation
Electricity charges
Water supply
Internet access (Wi-Fi)
Common area maintenance

Mess charges are collected separately and may vary depending on the semester.

Room Allotment

Hostel rooms are allotted by the Hostel Administration.

Students must:

Occupy only the allotted room.
Keep the room clean and hygienic.
Avoid unauthorized room exchanges.
Report any maintenance issues to the hostel office.

Room changes are permitted only with prior approval from the Hostel Warden.

Hostel Timings

Students are expected to follow the prescribed timings.

Hostel gate closes at 10:00 PM.
Entry after the closing time requires prior permission from the Hostel Warden.
Students leaving the hostel must make the necessary entry in the hostel register whenever required.

Repeated violations of hostel timings may lead to disciplinary action.

Visitor Policy

Visitors are permitted only during the approved visiting hours.

Visitor Timings:

4:00 PM to 7:00 PM

Visitors must:

Register at the security desk.
Carry a valid government-issued identity card.
Meet students only in the designated visitor area.

Visitors are not permitted inside student rooms.

Hostel Rules

Students must:

Carry their hostel ID cards at all times.
Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas.
Respect fellow residents and hostel staff.
Switch off electrical appliances when not in use.
Follow instructions issued by the Hostel Warden.

Students are responsible for the safety of their personal belongings.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

Ragging in any form
Smoking inside hostel premises
Consumption of alcohol or illegal substances
Gambling
Possession of dangerous weapons
Damaging hostel property
Creating excessive noise during study hours
Allowing unauthorized persons to stay overnight

Ragging is strictly prohibited and punishable under college regulations and applicable laws.

Hostel Facilities

The hostel provides the following facilities:

Furnished Rooms
Wi-Fi Connectivity
Study Rooms
Reading Hall
Dining Hall
Laundry Area
Clean Drinking Water
24×7 Security
CCTV Surveillance
First Aid Facility
Recreation Room

Students are expected to use these facilities responsibly.

Mess Rules

Students using the hostel mess must:

Follow the mess timings.
Maintain cleanliness in the dining area.
Avoid wasting food.
Report food-related issues to the Mess Committee.

Special meals may be arranged during festivals and college events.

Leave Policy

Students planning to leave the hostel must:

Obtain prior permission from the Hostel Warden.
Submit a leave application.
Inform their parents or guardians if required.
Record their departure and return in the hostel register.

Emergency leave requests will be handled on a case-by-case basis.

Discipline and Penalties

Violation of hostel rules may lead to disciplinary action.

Disciplinary measures may include:

Written Warning
Monetary Fine
Temporary Suspension from Hostel
Permanent Cancellation of Hostel Accommodation
Referral to the College Disciplinary Committee

Serious violations may result in additional academic or legal action.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is hostel accommodation available for both boys and girls?

Yes. Separate hostel facilities are available for both boys and girls.

What time does the hostel gate close?

The hostel gate closes at 10:00 PM.

When are visitors allowed?

Visitors are allowed only between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM.

Is ragging allowed inside the hostel?

No. Ragging is strictly prohibited and attracts strict disciplinary action.

Are students required to carry their hostel ID cards?

Yes. Students must carry their hostel ID cards at all times while staying in the hostel.

Are mess charges included in the hostel fee?

No. Mess charges are collected separately unless otherwise notified by the college.
